About our Smart Bikes

At Velocity Cycles, we're more than just a bike shop we're a community of cycling enthusiasts dedicated to providing high-quality bikes, expert services, and unmatched customer care. Whether you're a commuter, a weekend warrior, or a pro rider, we're here to keep you rolling.

Your Ride. Our Passion.

Founded in 1990 by two lifelong cyclists, Velocity Cycles began as a small workshop in a garage. Driven by a love for two wheels and the open road, we've grown into a trusted local brand known for premium bikes and personalized service. Our goal? To make cycling accessible, exciting, and sustainable for everyone.
